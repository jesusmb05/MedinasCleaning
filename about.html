About Medinas Cleaning LLC:

Serving the Twin Cities Area with Professional Care

Medinas Cleaning LLC is a professional cleaning company located in the Twin Cities Area of Minnesota. We proudly serve residents and businesses throughout the Twin Cities and surrounding areas.

Our team at Medinas Cleaning LLC offers a wide range of cleaning services tailored to meet each client's needs. We know the importance of having a clean, welcoming space — and we’re here to make that possible for you.

All of our employees are fully insured and professionally trained to handle everything from routine cleanings to deep sanitation jobs. Here at Medinas Cleaning, we provide services such as:

  • Residential Cleaning
  • Commercial & Office Cleaning
  • Move-In / Move-Out Cleaning
  • Post-Construction Cleaning
  • Window Washing
  • Carpet & Floor Cleaning
  • Custom Cleaning Plans
